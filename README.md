🔗 **Live Application:** https://kd1091bhavimani-ui.github.io/psh/

---

## ✨ Features

### 📊 Tax Calculator
- ✅ **Month-based DA Calculation** - 12.25% (Mar-Jun) | 14.25% (Jul-Feb)
- ✅ **Auto-calculated Allowances** - DA, HRA (7.5%), NPS (10%)
- ✅ **Both Tax Regimes** - New (₹75K standard) & Old (₹50K standard)
- ✅ **Tax Rebate u/s 87A** - ₹25,000 (up to ₹8 lakh income)
- ✅ **Professional Tax** - Karnataka PT (₹200/month)
- ✅ **80C Auto-calculation** - From GPF/NPS + LIC

### 👥 Employee Dashboard
- ✅ **Multi-employee Management** - Add, edit, delete employees
- ✅ **Smart Pre-filling** - Saves all salary data automatically
- ✅ **Quick Actions** - Calculate, Pay Slip, Form 16 from dashboard
- ✅ **Search Functionality** - Find by Name or KGID
- ✅ **Status Tracking** - Calculated, Form 16 generated, Data saved

### 💵 Pay Slip Generator
- ✅ **Monthly Pay Slips** - Professional format
- ✅ **Month Selection** - March to February (FY cycle)
- ✅ **Complete Breakdown** - Earnings & Deductions
- ✅ **Amount in Words** - Indian numbering system
- ✅ **Download/Share** - HTML format, Google Drive compatible

### 📄 Form 16 with Annexure
- ✅ **Complete Form 16** - As per Income Tax Act
- ✅ **Month-wise Statement** - March to February breakdown
- ✅ **Salary Components** - Basic, DA, HRA, MA, Allowances
- ✅ **Deduction Details** - PT, GIS, KGID, LIC, GPF/NPS
- ✅ **DDO Details** - Office name, TAN, signatures
- ✅ **Download Options** - Save to Drive or download

## 💡 Auto-Calculations

```
✓ DA = (Basic Pay × 12.25%) for Mar-Jun
✓ DA = (Basic Pay × 14.25%) for Jul-Feb  
✓ HRA = (Basic Pay × 7.5%)
✓ NPS = ((Basic + DA) × 10%) if NPS selected
```
